Educational Disclaimer

NutriScope is an educational tool, not a medical device. All calculations (BMR, calorie targets, macro and micronutrient targets, deficiency/excess flags, and risk analysis) are estimates based on generalized population formulas and standard reference intakes. They do not account for individual medical conditions, medications, lab results, pregnancy, lactation, or clinically diagnosed deficiencies.

Nutrient values for foods are typical/average figures and can vary significantly based on brand, preparation method, ripeness, and portion estimation. Do not use this tool to diagnose, treat, or manage any medical condition. The "Risk Analysis" section identifies patterns in your logged intake only — it is not a screening or diagnostic result.

Always consult a registered dietitian, nutritionist, or physician before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you have an existing health condition, are pregnant or breastfeeding, are underweight/overweight, or are managing a chronic illness.

Nutrition Reference Sources

Food composition values are approximated from publicly available nutrition references including:
• Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) — Nutritive Value of Indian Foods
• USDA FoodData Central
• National Institutes of Health (NIH) Office of Dietary Supplements — Vitamin & Mineral Fact Sheets
• Indian Council of Medical Research — Recommended Dietary Allowances (RDA) for Indians

Calorie and macro targets use the Mifflin-St Jeor BMR equation with standard Harris-Benedict-style activity multipliers. Micronutrient targets approximate adult RDAs and may differ from your personal medical guidance.
